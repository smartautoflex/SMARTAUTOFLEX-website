Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SMARTAUTOFLEX AI solutions and how they can transform your hotel operations.

How does AI room booking work? +

Our AI system integrates with your hotel's reservation database and uses natural language processing to understand customer requests. Guests can book rooms via voice call, chat, or web interface, and the AI handles availability checks, pricing, and confirmation automatically. The system learns from each interaction to improve future responses and can handle complex requests like room preferences, special accommodations, and package deals.

Can the system handle multiple customer calls at the same time? +

Yes, SMARTAUTOFLEX is designed to handle unlimited simultaneous customer interactions. The AI scales automatically based on demand, ensuring every customer receives prompt attention without wait times. Our cloud-based architecture ensures consistent performance even during peak check-in/check-out times or special events. Each interaction is processed independently while maintaining context and personalization.

Which languages are supported? +

Our system supports English plus regional languages based on your plan. The Starter Plan includes English + 1 regional language. The Comfort Plan includes 4 languages (English + 3 regional languages). The Elite Plan offers customized multi-language support for more than 4 languages, including less commonly spoken languages based on your hotel's clientele. All plans include continuous language model improvements.

Is human staff required to operate the system? +

No, SMARTAUTOFLEX operates completely autonomously 24/7. However, we recommend having staff available for exceptional situations that may require human intervention, though our AI handles over 95% of routine inquiries and tasks. The system includes an escalation protocol that can alert human staff when necessary. Most hotels report reducing front desk staffing by 60-80% while improving service quality.

Can the system be customized for our specific hotel needs? +

Absolutely. Our Elite Plan offers complete customization, including integration with your existing systems, customized workflows, and specialized features tailored to your hotel's unique requirements. We work closely with your team during implementation to ensure the system reflects your brand voice, handles your specific room types and packages, and integrates with your property management system, payment gateways, and other hotel technologies.

How does the system handle payment processing? +

SMARTAUTOFLEX integrates with major payment gateways and supports secure payment processing for room bookings, additional services, and incidentals. The system is PCI-DSS compliant and uses tokenization to ensure payment security. Guests can pay via credit/debit cards, digital wallets, or other payment methods supported by your hotel. All transactions are encrypted and recorded in your PMS with detailed audit trails.

Powered by n8n Workflow Automation

SMARTAUTOFLEX leverages n8n (pronounced "n-eight-n"), a powerful workflow automation tool that enables us to create complex automation scenarios without writing extensive code. n8n is a fair-code licensed workflow automation tool that can be self-hosted or used as a SaaS solution. Key benefits include:

Robust Integration

Seamlessly connects with your existing hotel management systems, CRMs, payment gateways, communication platforms, and IoT devices through 200+ built-in nodes and custom API integrations.

Visual Workflow Builder

Create and modify automation workflows with an intuitive visual interface, no coding required. Drag-and-drop nodes to design complex workflows that match your hotel's specific operational processes.

Enterprise Scalability

Easily scale automation processes as your hotel grows or requirements change. n8n supports high-volume workflows, parallel execution, and distributed architecture for enterprise-level deployments.

Data Ownership & Privacy

With self-hosted deployment options, you maintain complete control over your data. All guest information, booking data, and operational workflows remain within your infrastructure, ensuring GDPR and data privacy compliance.
